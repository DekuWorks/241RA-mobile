‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a sighting?

To report a sighting, tap on a case from the home screen, then tap "Report Sighting". You can add photos, location, and detailed information about what you observed.

How do I change my notification settings?

Go to your Profile screen, then tap "Notification Settings". You can enable/disable push notifications and email alerts.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your Profile screen, scroll down, and tap "Delete Account". Please note this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Why isn't my location showing correctly?

Make sure location services are enabled for the 241 Runners app in your device settings. The app needs location permission to show cases near you and allow you to report sightings.

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your Profile screen and tap "Edit" next to the information you want to update. Tap "Save" when you're finished.

I'm having trouble signing in. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  • Make sure you have an internet connection
  • Try signing in with a different method (Email, Google, or Apple)
  • Reset your password if using email sign-in
  • Contact support if the problem persists
